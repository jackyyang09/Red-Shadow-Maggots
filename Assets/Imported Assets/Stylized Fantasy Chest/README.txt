README

- In the exmple Scene the IRON chest is animated to open, to make your own animation for opening of the chest you have 
to rotate the top part of the chest on its pivot by -90 degrees or more.

- There are textures for Standart PBR Unity Shaders, use the Albedo, Normal, Metallic and AO. Master texture is 
used by the custom shader.

- To change the colors of the Crystal first select the "sf_chest_GOLD_Evil" or "sf_chest_IRON_Evil" material in Folder Materials, 
and move the slider Crystal Color.

- Custom Shaders are made in LWRP and tested here only.

- You can see the custom shaders in folder "Shaders". To open them Download the 
Shader Graph from Package Manager inside Unity and just open it.
